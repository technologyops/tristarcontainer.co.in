Tristar offers Accommodation / Office units and Housing options. These containers can have customized fit-outs and modifications to suit a number of requirements. Our range of equipment is designed & developed based on standard ISO specifications & conditions.

Highlights:

  • Windows, roll-up/swing - out doors
  • Roof ventilation turbines/ skylights
  • Custom painting and logos
  • High security door locks and window bars
  • Shelving and built-in cupboards
  • On-site offices, workshops and showrooms
  • Retail outlets

Our versatile buildings require no foundations or structural assembly. They are ideal for shelters, temporary or permanent living quarters, portable offices/studios, mining camps, construction housing/field offices, worker housing, portable emergency clinics, storm shelters, military/police posts, forest/mountain cabins, etc.

Rugged and durable, our units are perfectly suited for disaster relief, emergency housing, and other harsh conditions.

Our cabins can be transported via standard cargo container ships, trucks or rail.
