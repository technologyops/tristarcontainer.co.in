Reefer Containers

Built with high quality materials and lined with high grade stainless steel, Tristar reefer containers are available in various sizes, including 20´ and 40´ High Cube and serve as ideal storage and transport for:

  • Milk, cheese, yogurt, butter, ice-creams and all other dairy products
  • Fresh and frozen meats, with optional racks supplied to facilitate the hanging of medium sized carcasses
  • The container can be made suitable for fresh and frozen marine products
  • Horticulture and floriculture products
  • Beverages and heat sensitive chemicals

Insulated Containers

Insulated storage containers boast of an extremely heavy insulation to preserve perishable goods. Products that are sensitive to temperature changes such as food, chemicals and pharmaceutical products, may be transported or stored onsite with utmost preservation and security.
